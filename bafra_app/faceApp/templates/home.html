Receive your username and password

Step 1: You must receive a username and password, to be able to connect to the application, if you already have your username and password you can go to step 2. If not contact the support or the administrator to receive your identifiers

Login

Step 2: After having received your username and password, you must connect to the application via the login button or this link, and enter your username and password

Reset your password

Step 3: After logging into your account, you must change your password, by clicking on account -> update or via this link

Save a video

Step 4: After changing your password, you must take a live video and record it on the application, the video must be very well taken and must be of one minute duration.
